Refund Policies

  • Refunds will only be made if the festival is cancelled. If the festival is canceled due to unforeseen circumstances, ticket holders will receive a full refund.
  • Refunds will only be issued for tickets that have not been used and scanned for admission to the festival. If you purchase a ticket but do not attend the festival, you may be eligible for a refund, subject to festival policies.
  • Refunds may be issued in the event of emergencies or extenuating circumstances, such as serious illness or family emergencies. Documents may be required in such circumstances to receive a refund.
  • There are no refunds for items purchased at the festival, including merchandise, food and drink tickets. These sales are final.
