Venue Information

Welcome to the FutureWorld Music Festival! This year's event takes place at the beautiful Lake Führinger. The sprawling scenic lake is perfect for a music festival with multiple stages, a large main stage for headliners, and plenty of room for attendees to enjoy the performance. The site also has top-notch facilities, including clean restrooms, food vendors, and plenty of shaded areas to relax. Additionally, the festival will have a dedicated team of security guards and medical staff on-site to ensure the safety and health of all attendees. We can't wait to experience the magic of music festivals in this incredible place!
