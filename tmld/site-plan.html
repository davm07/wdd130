Overview

Purpose

Provide information about the event, dates, lineup and dj's for fans and attendees of the Tomorrowland AM music festival, also giving them some resources to plan their trip to the festival.

Audience

The target audience would primary consist of people who are interested in EDM (Electronic Dance Music), people who likes to attend to music festivals, as well as as those who are looking for a unique and exciting travel experience. Overall the audience would likely be divided towards people in theirs 20s and 30s, but would still have a diverse mix of age groups.

Content

Home page

Lineup and schedule
Here you can find specific information about the lineup and schedule. The lineup and schedule are two of the most important features that you want to know about this electronic music festival. These sections will provide information about the artists and DJs who will be performing, as well as the dates and times of each performance.
Artist profiles: Attendees are likely to be passionate about the artists and DJs who are performing at the festival, and they'll want to know as much as possible about them. Each artist’s profile would include a photo, a brief bio, and a list of their most popular tracks. Some of the artists that will be included in this section will be David Guetta, Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, Martin Garrix, Diplo, Marshmello, and Armin Van Buuren.
Schedule: Once the festival lineup has been announced, many attendees will start planning out their schedules. Having a schedule is a great way to make this process easy and fun. Attendees should be able to get that information to create a personalized lineup by selecting the artists they want to see and adding them to their schedules. Also, it could offer features such as alerts or reminders for when each performance is about to start. This will help attendees stay organized and make the most of their time at the festival.

Tickets and admission

The process of buying tickets and gaining admission to an electronic music festival can be confusing and stressful, especially for first-time attendees. To ensure that everything runs smoothly, it's important to provide clear and concise information about tickets and admissions.
Packages: Many festivals offer different options to the attendees, this music festival would be divided into three different days, starting on Friday and ending on Sunday. This section will show the tickets only for one Day, tickets for the whole festival, and VIP packages. Every ticket will have different perks that will provide different experiences for the attendees. These packages might include access to special viewing areas, meet-and-greets with artists, or priority entry. Also, it could have a comparison chart to help attendees choose the package that's right for them, and for their willingness to pay.
Refund policies: Unfortunately, sometimes attendees need to cancel their plans or change their tickets. To avoid confusion and frustration, it's important to have clear refund policies in place. In a brief section, it will clearly communicate the policies for refunds, ticket transfers, cancellations, and time periods in which they can cancel their trip and tickets, and provide a customer support channel for attendees who have questions or concerns.

Event information

Even after attendees have bought their tickets and planned out their schedules, they'll still have questions about the festival itself. It's important to provide clear and comprehensive information about the event, including the location, safety policies, and recommended accommodations. Also, it is important to show them a background of the previous year’s experiences and activities, if they are new attendees, they will find this information pretty helpful.
Venue Information: The festival venue is one of the most important aspects of the event. Attendees will need to know how to get there, where to park, and what to expect when they arrive. This section will provide detailed information about the venue, including directions, transportation options, and recommended accommodations. It would also include information about nearby restaurants or attractions, to help attendees plan their off-hours activities.
Festival activities: While the music is the main event, the festival will have additional activities and attractions to keep attendees entertained throughout the day. These might include food vendors, merchandise booths, and games to play in groups or couples.
